Transit & Sustainability

Complete guide to stadium transportation and eco-friendly visitor tips

Stadium Transportation

  • NJ Transit Rail (Train)

    Arrives directly at the stadium transit center located immediately outside **Gate A (North Entrance)**. Service runs frequently on match days.

  • Express Shuttle Buses

    Departs from **Lot B (near Gate B)** connecting visitors to regional transit terminals. Buses run every 5-10 minutes starting 4 hours pre-match.

  • Parking lots A, B, C, D & E

    Lots A, B, C, D require pre-booked permits. Lot E (near Gate C) is open public parking on a first-come, first-served basis. Handi-cap access parking is available in Lot 3.

Sustainability Tips

  • Water Refill Stations

    Visitors are permitted to bring empty reusable water bottles. Free, high-velocity water refill hydration hubs are located at **Section 110** and **Section 220**.

  • Public Transit Benefits

    Help us reduce emissions! Travel via train or shuttle to reduce the carbon footprint of the tournament. Public transit riders get priority gate access.

  • Waste Sorting & Recycling

    Please use the correct receptacles: - Green Bins: Composting and food scraps. - Blue Bins: Clean recyclables (plastic cups, aluminum cans). - Black Bins: General waste.
